Thank you for volunteering at Matsuri!

Please read the instructions below before signing up for any shifts.
How to sign up
1
Fill out the Sign-Up Form & Waiver
Complete this once before selecting any shifts. This registers you and adds your name to the volunteer list.
2
Select your shifts in the spreadsheet
Enter your name in the time slots you want. Your name should appear in the dropdown after completing the form.
Please do not move or edit other people's names.
📋 1) Open Form & Waiver 📅 2) Open Sign-Up Sheet
